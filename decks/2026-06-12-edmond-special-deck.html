LAYER 5/OKLAHOMA TRAPS 01

Questions you didn't know to ask

/HAIL

One of America's most hail-prone regions: higher premiums, constant roof replacement, post-storm roofing scammers. "Newer roof" is a real selling point; a trusted roofer is a key vendor.

/CLAY SOIL

Expansive clay → foundation movement is common. Stair-step brick cracks and stuck doors = stop. A structural engineer costs a few hundred; a foundation surprise turns +$40k into −$10k.

20:09
LAYER 5/OKLAHOMA TRAPS 02

Questions you didn't know to ask

/VACANT HOUSES

Winterize or keep heat on: a burst pipe in a vacant flip is a five-figure event. Standard homeowner's policies often exclude vacancy → builder's risk + vacant policies.

/LANDLORD + TAXES

OK is landlord-friendly with relatively fast evictions, but verify everything your PM says in year one. Property tax ~0.9% effective, reassessed at YOUR purchase price.

RULES SHIFT · RIGHT QUESTIONS, NOT FINAL ANSWERS · VERIFY WITH YOUR LOCAL TEAM
