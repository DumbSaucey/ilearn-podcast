SECTION 3/WHY IT MATTERS

Unpermitted work = uninsured + unsellable

/THE INSURANCE TRAP

Unpermitted electrical or plumbing voids coverage on related losses. Your wire, no permit, a fire mid-rehab: the builder's risk policy can walk away.

/THE RESALE TRAP

The buyer's inspector WILL ask for permits on that new panel and repipe. Unpermitted work doesn't just risk fines: it un-sells the house.
